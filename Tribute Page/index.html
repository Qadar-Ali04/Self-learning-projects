Eren Yeager

"If you don't fight, you can't win."

Here is the time line of Eren Yeager's life:

845 - The Fall of Shiganshina

  • The Colossal Titan breaches Wall Maria.
  • Eren witnesses the death of his mother, Carla Yeager.
  • This moment defines his obsession with freedom and hatred of Titans.
  • Eren vows to eradicate every Titan.

847 - 850 - Training Corps Enrollment

  • Eren joins the 104th Training Corps alongside Mikasa and Armin.
  • Struggles with discipline but excels in determination and combat instinct.
  • Graduates with strong resolve rather than natural talent.

850 - Battle of Trost District

  • Eren sacrifices himself to save Armin.
  • Reveals the ability to transform into a Titan for the first time.
  • Becomes humanity’s most valuable and dangerous asset.

850 - Trial and Induction into the Survey Corps

  • Military debates Eren’s existence as a threat.
  • Levi takes responsibility for him.
  • Eren officially joins the Survey Corps.

850 - Female Titan Arc

  • Annie Leonhart revealed as the Female Titan.
  • Eren struggles with betrayal and hesitation.
  • Learns that enemies can be close allies.

851 - Clash of the Titans

  • Reiner and Bertholdt revealed as the Armored and Colossal Titans.
  • Eren discovers the Titans are humans from outside the walls.
  • Activates the Founding Titan’s power unintentionally.

851 - Uprising Arc

  • The truth of the royal government is revealed.
  • Historia Reiss becomes queen.
  • Eren learns about the Founding Titan and royal bloodline.

851 - Return to Shiganshina

  • Bertholdt and Reiner are defeated.
  • Erwin Smith dies.
  • The basement of Eren’s childhood home is unlocked.

851 - The Truth of the World

  • Grisha Yeager’s journals reveal Marley, Eldians, and the outside world.
  • Eren learns Titans are weapons in a global conflict.
  • His dream of freedom becomes morally conflicted.

854 - Crossing the Sea

  • The Scouts reach the ocean.
  • Eren realizes freedom lies beyond enemies, not walls.
  • His outlook becomes darker and more distant.

854–857 - Infiltration of Marley

  • Eren infiltrates Marley under the alias “Kruger.”
  • Attacks Liberio during a festival.
  • Kills civilians and gains the War Hammer Titan.

857 - War Against the World

  • Eren rejects diplomacy.
  • Manipulates allies and enemies alike.
  • Becomes viewed as a global threat.

857 - Activation of the Rumbling

  • Eren uses the Founding Titan’s full power.
  • Releases countless Colossal Titans.
  • Declares his intent to destroy most of humanity outside Paradis.

857 - Final Confrontation

  • Former allies form an alliance to stop Eren.
  • Mikasa delivers the final blow.
  • Eren’s death ends the Rumbling.

Aftermath- Legacy of Eren Yeager

  • Titans disappear from the world.
  • Peace remains fragile.
  • Eren is remembered as both a savior and a mass murderer.
  • His story raises questions about freedom, sacrifice, and destiny.
